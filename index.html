Who We Are

At Maramungala Technologies, we are dedicated to solving the everyday challenges of commuters through advanced technology and innovation. Founded by Rohith Venkatramulu Maramungala and Narendra Chigili, our company is built on a vision to transform public transportation into a digital-first experience.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To revolutionize public transportation in India by bridging the gap between public and private transport through digitalization, providing reliable, affordable, accessible and innovative mobility solutions for every commuter.

Our Mission

To enhance the daily travel experience through innovative technology, empowering commuters with real time information, cashless transactions, multi-modal connectivity between first and last mile, seamless navigation and integrated transportation services.

Why Work With Us?

Joining the team at Maramungala Technologies Private Limited means becoming an integral part of India's transportation revolution. Together, we're redefining the future of commuting through innovation and collaboration.

Growth

Meaningful Impact

Work-Life Balance

We understand the value of balance in life. While we work passionately to achieve our mission, we also encourage our team to prioritize personal well-being and strike a healthy work-life balance.

By joining us, you become a vital part of a dynamic team driving real change, while growing, learning, and thriving in a supportive and innovative environment.
